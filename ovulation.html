关于排卵期About Ovulation

排卵期是指卵子从卵巢释放出来的时期，通常在下次月经来潮前14天左右。排卵日前后5天为易孕期，此期间同房受孕几率最高。Ovulation is when the egg is released from the ovary, usually about 14 days before the next period. The fertile window is 5 days before and including ovulation day, when conception is most likely.
