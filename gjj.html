关于公积金About Housing Provident Fund

住房公积金是中国特有的住房保障制度，由职工和单位共同缴存，用于购房、租房、装修等住房消费。公积金贷款利率比商业贷款低很多，是购房的重要融资渠道。The Housing Provident Fund (HPF) is a China-specific housing security system where employees and employers contribute together, used for home purchases, rentals, and renovations. HPF loan rates are significantly lower than commercial loan rates.
