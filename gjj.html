关于公积金计算器

About Housing Provident Fund Calculator

公积金（住房公积金）是中国特有的住房保障制度，由职工和单位共同缴存，用于购房、租房、装修等住房消费。公积金贷款利率比商业贷款低很多，是购房的重要融资渠道。2026年，全国各地公积金政策有所调整，本计算器提供参考计算。

说起来，公积金可能是打工人最熟悉的"存钱罐"了——每个月工资条上都会扣掉一笔，单位再补一笔，相当于强制储蓄。对于打算买房的朋友，这笔钱用得好，能省下不少利息呢！😄

公积金缴存规则

公积金由职工和单位共同缴存，缴存基数为职工本人上一年度月平均工资，缴存比例在5%-12%之间由单位自行选择。个人缴存部分和单位缴存部分均存入职工个人账户，归职工所有。

公积金贷款优势

  • 利率低：2026年公积金贷款利率5年以上约3.1%，比商业贷款低约30%
  • 额度高：单人最高可贷60万，双人最高100万（因城市而异）
  • 可抵扣：公积金账户余额可抵扣房贷本金
  • 还款灵活：支持提前还款，无违约金

可贷额度计算规则

公积金贷款额度一般由以下因素决定：账户余额×倍数（通常为14-20倍）+ 缴存年限×固定额度。不同城市规则不同，本计算器按常见规则估算，实际额度以当地公积金中心审核为准。

常见问题

Q: 公积金可以取出来吗？

A: 可以。在购房、租房、装修、退休、离职等情况下可提取公积金。

Q: 换工作后公积金怎么办？

A: 公积金账户会自动转移合并，无需担心。

Q: 公积金贷款利率是多少？

A: 2026年，5年以下利率约2.6%，5年以上利率约3.1%。

The Housing Provident Fund (公积金, gōngjījīn) is a unique housing security system in China where both employees and employers contribute. It's designed for housing purchases, rentals, renovations, and other housing-related expenses. The interest rate on housing fund loans is significantly lower than commercial loans, making it a crucial financing option for homebuyers. This calculator provides reference calculations for 2026.

Honestly, the Housing Provident Fund might be the most familiar "piggy bank" for working people — every month, a portion is deducted from your salary, and your employer adds matching contributions. It's like a forced savings plan! For those planning to buy property, making good use of this fund can save you quite a bit on interest. 😄

Contribution Rules

The Housing Provident Fund is contributed by both the employee and employer. The contribution base is the employee's average monthly salary from the previous year. The contribution rate (typically between 5%-12%) is determined by the employer. Both the employee's and employer's contributions go into the employee's personal account, belonging entirely to the employee.

Advantages of Housing Fund Loans

  • Low Interest Rate: In 2026, rates for loans over 5 years are approximately 3.1%, about 30% lower than commercial loans
  • High Loan Amount: Up to 600,000 CNY for individuals, up to 1,000,000 CNY for couples (varies by city)
  • Can Offset Principal: Account balance can be used to offset mortgage principal
  • Flexible Repayment: Early repayment supported with no penalty

Maximum Loan Amount Calculation

The housing fund loan amount is generally determined by: Account Balance × Multiple (usually 14-20x) + Years of Contribution × Fixed Amount. Rules vary by city. This calculator estimates based on common rules — actual amounts are subject to your local housing fund center's approval.

FAQ

Q: Can I withdraw my Housing Provident Fund?

A: Yes. You can withdraw funds when purchasing property, renting, renovating, retiring, or changing jobs.

Q: What happens to my Housing Provident Fund when I change jobs?

A: Your account transfers automatically — no need to worry!

Q: What's the interest rate for Housing Provident Fund loans in 2026?

A: In 2026, rates are approximately 2.6% for terms under 5 years, and approximately 3.1% for terms over 5 years.
