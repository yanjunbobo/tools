关于安全期计算器 · About Period Tracker

安全期计算器根据你的月经周期推算排卵期和安全期。Whether you're trying to conceive or looking to avoid pregnancy, this tool helps you understand your body better.

什么是安全期？· What Are Safe Days?

安全期是指女性不易怀孕的时期。传统来说，月经来潮前后一周相对安全。Safe days refer to the time when pregnancy is less likely to occur. Generally, the week before and after your period is considered relatively safe — but here's the thing, it's not foolproof.

安全期为什么不可靠？· Why Are Safe Days Unreliable?

  • 排卵受情绪、压力、环境影响大 · Ovulation is easily affected by stress, emotions, and environment
  • 精子在体内可存活3-5天 · Sperm can survive in the body for 3-5 days
  • 月经不规律者无法准确计算 · Irregular cycles make calculation inaccurate
  • 个体差异大，安全期因人而异 · Everyone's body is different

推荐的避孕方式 · Recommended Contraception

安全期避孕失败率约20%，不建议单独使用。Safe days alone have about a 20% failure rate — not great odds if you're trying to avoid pregnancy. 可信赖的避孕方式：

  • 避孕套（正确使用成功率98%）· Condoms (98% effective when used correctly)
  • 短效避孕药（成功率99%）· Birth control pills (99% effective)
  • 宫内节育器（成功率99%）· IUD (99% effective)

常见问题 · FAQ

Q: 安全期会怀孕吗？· Can I get pregnant during safe days?

A: 会。安全期避孕失败率约20%，因为排卵可能提前或推迟，且精子可在体内存活数日。Yes, it's possible. The failure rate is about 20% because ovulation can shift, and sperm can live inside you for days.

Q: 月经期同房会怀孕吗？· Can I get pregnant during my period?

A: 月经期一般不会排卵，但月经期同房容易引起感染，且排卵可能提前，不建议。Unlikely, but月经期同房容易引起感染，不建议。While pregnancy is unlikely during your period, it's not a good idea due to infection risks.

Q: 怎么知道自己的排卵期？· How can I track ovulation?

A: 可使用排卵试纸、基础体温法、观察宫颈黏液、B超监测等方法。You can use ovulation test strips, track basal body temperature, monitor cervical mucus, or get an ultrasound.
