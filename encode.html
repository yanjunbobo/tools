❓ 常见问题FAQ

Q: 为什么要URL编码？Why URL encode?

A: URL中有些字符有特殊含义（如?、&、/），有些字符无法直接传输（如中文）。URL编码将它们转换为%开头格式，确保URL正确解析。Some URL characters have special meanings (? & /), and some characters like Chinese cannot be transmitted directly. URL encoding converts them to %-prefixed format to ensure correct URL parsing.

Q: Base64能加密吗？Can Base64 encrypt?

A: 不能。Base64只是编码格式，任何人都可以反向解码，没有安全性可言。如需加密请使用AES、RSA等专业加密算法。No. Base64 is just an encoding format — anyone can reverse it. For security, use professional encryption algorithms like AES or RSA.

Q: 我的数据安全吗？Is my data safe?

A: 完全安全。所有转换操作都在您的浏览器本地完成，我们不会收集、存储或传输您的任何数据。Completely safe. All conversions run locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

Q: 支持批量转换吗？Does it support batch conversion?

A: 支持。直接粘贴大段文本即可批量转换，没有数量限制。Yes. Paste large amounts of text directly for batch conversion with no quantity limit.

Q: Unicode和UTF-8有什么区别？What's the difference between Unicode and UTF-8?

A: Unicode是字符集，给每个字符分配唯一编号。UTF-8是编码规则，将Unicode编号转换为字节序列。\\u是Unicode的转义表示形式。Unicode is a character set that assigns a unique number to each character. UTF-8 is an encoding rule that converts Unicode numbers to byte sequences. \\u is an escaped representation of Unicode.
