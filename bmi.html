💡 Did you know? | 你知道吗？
BMI (Body Mass Index) is the most widely used indicator to assess whether your weight is healthy. The Chinese BMI standard is stricter than the Western one — because people of Asian descent tend to carry more body fat at the same BMI level.

BMI是什么？
What is BMI?
BMI (Body Mass Index，身体质量指数) is a simple calculation using your weight and height. It gives you a rough idea of whether you're at a healthy weight. It's not perfect — it doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat — but it's a good starting point for most people.

📐 Formula | 计算公式

BMI = Weight(kg) ÷ Height(m)²

Example | 例如：65kg ÷ (1.7m)² = 65 ÷ 2.89 ≈ 22.5

🇨🇳 Chinese vs. Western Standards | 中国与欧美标准对比

Here's something many people don't realize: the Chinese BMI standard is different from the Western one.中国人的体质和欧美人不同，在相同BMI下，中国人往往有更高的体脂率。因此：中国制定了更严格的参考标准：

  • 偏瘦 Underweight：BMI < 18.5 — Might indicate nutritional deficiency | 可能有营养不良风险
  • 正常 Normal：BMI 18.5 - 23.9 — Great! Keep it up! | 很好！保持健康生活方式
  • 超重 Overweight：BMI 24 - 27.9 — A little extra — consider more movement | 轻度超重，建议适当运动
  • 肥胖 Obese：BMI ≥ 28 — Worth paying attention to | 值得重视，建议积极干预

🔥 BMR — Your Base Metabolic Rate | 基础代谢率

What is BMR?
BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body burns just to stay alive — breathing, circulating blood, growing cells, everything. Think of it as the calories you'd burn if you just lay in bed all day doing nothing.

Why does it matter?
Knowing your BMR helps you plan meals intelligently. If you eat roughly your BMR worth of food and exercise to burn extra calories, you're on a solid track for weight management.

Formulas | 公式：

Men | 男性：66.47 + 13.75 × weight + 5.003 × height(cm) - 6.755 × age

Women | 女性：655.1 + 9.563 × weight + 1.850 × height(cm) - 4.676 × age

🎯 Ideal Weight | 理想体重

We calculate your ideal weight using BMI = 22 as the target — this is the BMI value associated with the lowest health risks for most people.

Ideal Weight = 22 × Height(m)²

⚠️ Limitations of BMI | BMI的局限性

Here's the truth about BMI — it has blind spots:

  • It can't tell muscle from fat. A bodybuilder might score "obese" even with low body fat | 分不清肌肉和脂肪，健身达人的BMI可能偏高
  • It doesn't account for age-related muscle loss in seniors | 不能反映老年人肌肉流失
  • It doesn't measure where fat is stored. Belly fat is riskier than hip fat | 不能区分脂肪存储位置，腹部脂肪更危险

💡 Pro tip: Combine BMI with waist circumference for a clearer picture of your health.
