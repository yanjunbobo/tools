五险一金是什么？What are Five Insurance and One Fund?

五险（Five Insurance）：Five Insurance:

  • 养老保险Pension — 老了退休了有钱领Income after retirement
  • 医疗保险Medical Insurance — 看病能报销Medical expense reimbursement
  • 失业保险Unemployment Insurance — 失业了能领补贴Subsidy when unemployed
  • 工伤保险Work Injury Insurance — 工作受伤有保障Protection for work injuries
  • 生育保险Maternity Insurance — 生孩子能报销Expenses for childbirth

一金（One Fund）：One Fund: 住房公积金Housing Provident Fund — 买房贷款利率低，租房也可以提取Lower mortgage rates, can withdraw for rent
