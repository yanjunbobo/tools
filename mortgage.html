买房前必看的还款攻略Repayment Guide Before Buying

我自己买第一套房的时候，光是研究还款方式就头疼了好几天。等额本息和等额本金到底差多少？公积金能贷多少？组合贷合算吗？这些问题我都遇到过。今天把经验分享出来，希望能帮大家少走弯路。When I bought my first home, I spent days researching repayment methods. Equal principal and interest vs equal principal — what's the difference? How much HPF loan can I get? Is a combo loan worth it? I'm sharing my experience to help you avoid detours.

等额本息 vs 等额本金，我该怎么选？Equal Principal & Interest vs Equal Principal — Which to Pick?

等额本息：每月还款金额固定，前期还得主要是利息，本金还得慢。适合收入稳定、不想前期压力太大的上班族。Equal Principal & Interest: Fixed monthly payment, early repayments are mostly interest. Suitable for salaried workers who want stable monthly payments without high upfront pressure.

等额本金：每月本金固定，利息逐月减少，所以前期月供高、后期月供低。总利息比等额本息少约20%-30%，但前期还款压力大。Equal Principal: Principal is fixed monthly, interest decreases over time, so early payments are high and late payments are low. Total interest ~20-30% less than equal principal & interest, but early repayment pressure is higher.

举例：贷款100万、20年、商贷3.6%Example: ¥1M loan, 20yr, commercial rate 3.6%

  • 等额本息：月供约5736元，总利息约37.7万Equal Principal & Interest: ~¥5,736/month, total interest ~¥377k
  • 等额本金：首月约7167元，末月约4188元，总利息约30.1万Equal Principal: 1st month ~¥7,167, last month ~¥4,188, total interest ~¥301k

如果手里有闲钱、或者收入会稳步增长，等额本金更划算。如果追求月供稳定、留点余钱投资，等额本息更合适。If you have idle funds or expect steady income growth, equal principal is better. If you prefer stable payments and keeping cash for investment, equal principal & interest is more suitable.

公积金能贷多少？How Much HPF Loan Can I Get?

公积金贷款额度 = 账户余额 × 15 + 缴存年限 × 10000（各地规则不同）HPF loan limit = Account Balance × 15 + Payment Years × 10,000 (rules vary by city)

以北京为例，单人最高60万，双人最高120万。但很多城市如果二套房，额度会打折扣。In Beijing, max ¥600k for individual, ¥1.2M for couples. Many cities reduce limits for second homes.

我的建议：能贷公积金就贷公积金，利率比商贷低一大截。但要注意公积金贷款审核比较严格，征信、负债都有要求。My advice: Use HPF if you can — the rate is significantly lower than commercial loans. Note that HPF loans have strict credit and debt checks.

组合贷到底合不合算？Is Combo Loan Worth It?

组合贷就是一部分公积金+一部分商贷。适合公积金贷款额度不够用的情况。Combo loan = part HPF + part commercial. Suitable when HPF amount is insufficient.

比如要贷款150万，公积金最多贷60万，剩下的90万走商贷。虽然商贷部分利率高，但比纯商贷还是要省一些利息。E.g., ¥1.5M loan: HPF max ¥600k, remaining ¥900k commercial. Though commercial portion is higher rate, it still saves interest vs full commercial.

还款小技巧Repayment Tips

  • 提前还款：Early repayment: 公积金贷款提前还款没违约金，商贷大部分也没有，但要注意合同约定HPF loans usually have no early repayment penalty; most commercial loans don't either, but check your contract
  • 商贷转公积金：Commercial-to-HPF conversion: 如果之前纯商贷，后来公积金缴存达标，可以申请商转公，能省不少利息If you had pure commercial loan and now qualify for HPF, you can convert to save significant interest
  • 还款日：Repayment date: 建议设在发工资之后几天，避免忘了还影响征信Set it a few days after payday to avoid missed payments affecting your credit

常见问题FAQ

Q: 贷款100万30年月供多少？How much is ¥1M for 30 years monthly payment?

A: 按2026年商贷利率3.6%等额本息计算，月供约4558元，总利息约64万。At 2026 commercial rate 3.6% equal principal & interest: ~¥4,558/month, total interest ~¥640k.

Q: 等额本息第几年还最划算？In which year is early repayment most worthwhile for equal principal & interest?

A: 前5年提前还款最划算，因为这时候利息占比最高，本金还得少。First 5 years — interest portion is highest and principal reduction is minimal.

Q: 收入证明不够怎么办？What if my income proof is insufficient?

A: 可以让配偶作为共同借款人，或者提供其他资产证明。Add your spouse as co-borrower, or provide additional asset proof.
