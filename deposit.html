关于存款利息计算器 | About Deposit Interest Calculator

银行存钱大概是普通人最熟悉的"理财"方式了吧？虽然利息确实不高，但胜在稳——钱放进去基本不会少，适合当压舱石。2026年了，各家银行利率还是有差异的，用这个计算器帮你货比三家，看看存钱到底能赚多少利息。

Bank deposits are probably the most familiar "investment" for ordinary people. The interest rate is indeed not high, but the advantage is stability—your money is safe. In 2026, interest rates still vary between banks. Use this calculator to compare and see how much interest your savings can earn.

💵 活期存款 / Demand Deposits

活期就是想存就存、想取就取，灵活度拉满，但利率也是最低的。大银行一般就0.3%左右，有些小银行可能稍微高一点点。我的建议是：留够3-6个月生活费当应急钱就行，多了真没必要放活期。

Demand deposits offer maximum flexibility—you can deposit and withdraw anytime. But the interest rate is the lowest, around 0.3% at major banks, slightly higher at some smaller ones. My advice: keep 3-6 months of living expenses as emergency fund, don't leave more than that in demand deposits.

📅 定期存款 / Term Deposits

定期就是约定期限，存3个月/6个月/1年/2年/3年/5年不等。存得越久利率越高，这个应该挺好理解的。需要注意的是：如果中途提前支取，利息会按活期利率算，等于白存了。所以存之前先想好这笔钱短期内用不用得上。

Term deposits lock your money for a set period: 3 months, 6 months, 1 year, 2 years, 3 years, or 5 years. Longer terms mean higher rates. Important note: if you withdraw early, interest is calculated at demand deposit rates—you essentially lose all the extra interest. So think carefully about whether you'll need this money before the term ends.

🏆 大额存单 / Certificates of Deposit (CDs)

大额存单是银行发行的大额存款凭证，起购门槛20万。利率比普通定期高，而且支持转让，流动性比普通定期好一些。如果你有一笔较大的闲置资金，短期内用不上，大额存单是个不错的稳定收益选择。

CDs are large-denomination deposit certificates issued by banks, with a minimum purchase of 200,000 CNY. Rates are higher than regular term deposits, and they can be transferred, giving better liquidity. If you have a sizable sum that you won't need short-term, CDs are a good stable-yield option.

🧮 利息计算公式 / Interest Calculation Formula

利息 = 本金 × 年利率 × 存期（年）

Interest = Principal × Annual Rate × Term (Years)

举例：存10万、1年、年利率2%，利息 = 100000 × 2% × 1 = 2000元

Example: Deposit 100,000 CNY for 1 year at 2% annual rate → Interest = 100,000 × 2% × 1 = 2,000 CNY

❓ 常见问题 / FAQ

Q: 10万存银行一年利息多少？| Q: How much interest does 100K CNY earn in one year?

A: 按年利率2%计算，10万存1年利息约2000元。不同银行利率不一样，建议多比较几家。

A: At 2% annual rate, 100,000 CNY earns about 2,000 CNY interest in one year. Rates vary between banks—worth shopping around.

Q: 定期存款提前取会怎样？| Q: What happens if I withdraw a term deposit early?

A: 定期存款提前支取按活期利率算，利息会大打折扣。建议合理规划存期，或者用阶梯存款法分散风险。

A: Early withdrawal converts your term deposit to demand deposit rates, significantly reducing earned interest. Plan your terms carefully, or use a laddering strategy to spread risk.

Q: 存款保险是什么意思？| Q: What is deposit insurance?

A: 简单说就是：你在一家银行的存款，50万以内的部分受到国家存款保险制度保护。即使银行破产，这部分钱也能全额拿回来。超过50万的话，就要看银行清算结果了。

A: Simply put: deposits up to 500,000 CNY per bank are protected by national deposit insurance. If the bank goes bankrupt, you still get this amount back in full. Anything above 500K depends on the bank's liquidation.

Q: 怎样存款利息最高？| Q: How to maximize deposit interest?

A: 几个小技巧：①小银行利率通常比大银行高；②大额存单利率优于普通定期；③分散存不同期限（阶梯存款）；④关注银行季末/年末的加息活动

A: Some tips: ① Smaller banks often offer higher rates than big banks; ② CDs rate better than regular term deposits; ③ Spread across different terms (laddering strategy); ④ Watch for rate promotions at quarter/year-end
