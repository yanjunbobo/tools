💡 什么是专项附加扣除？💡 What Are "Special Additional Deductions"?

2019年个税改革后，国家给咱们发福利啦！简单说就是在5000元基本减除费用基础上，符合条件的还能再减一部分。关键是好消息：不用自己准备证明材料，单位每月直接帮你扣掉，省心！

Since the 2019 tax reform, the government introduced these deductions to ease our tax burden. Essentially: on top of the ¥5,000 basic deduction, you can subtract more if you qualify. The best part? Your employer handles it automatically — no paperwork needed!

🎁 年终奖怎么算税？🎁 How Is Year-End Bonus Taxed?

年终奖有两种计税方式：单独计税和合并计税。单独计税是把年终奖除以12个月，按月收入单独算；合并计税是加到全年综合所得里一起算。

Two options: separate taxation (divide bonus by 12, apply monthly rate) or combined taxation (add to annual income).

一般来说：月薪<1万的单独计税更划算；月薪>3万的合并计税可能更省。具体怎么选，用计算器分别试试就知道啦！

Rule of thumb: if your monthly salary is under ¥10k, separate is usually better. If it's over ¥30k, combined might save you more. When in doubt, calculate both ways!
