💡 关于密码安全 / Password Security Tips

谁也不想自己的账号被人盗了对吧？一个好密码就像一把好锁——看起来不起眼，但真的能挡住大部分麻烦。这个工具帮你生成足够强、足够随机的密码，你只需要记住一件事：每个账号用独立密码。

Don't want your account hacked? A good password is like a sturdy lock—it looks simple but stops most troublemakers. This tool generates strong, random passwords. Just remember: one account, one password.

什么样的密码最靠谱？/ What Makes a Password Strong?

  • 长度至少12位 / At least 12 characters: 每多一位，破解难度翻倍都不止 / Every extra character multiplies the difficulty
  • 大小写混合 / Mix upper and lower case: 让字符空间从26变成52 / Expands your character space from 26 to 52
  • 塞点数字和符号 / Add numbers & symbols: 就像给密码加了几道暗门 / Adds a few hidden doors for hackers
  • 别用生日、名字、电话 / Skip personal info: 这些太好猜了 / Too easy to guess
  • 每个地方密码不一样 / Unique passwords everywhere: 万一泄露一个，不影响其他 / One leak won't compromise everything

密码熵值是什么鬼？/ What's Password Entropy?

简单说，熵值就是密码的"随机程度"。越随机，熵值越高，破解难度越大。不用深究，知道这个就够了：

Simply put, entropy measures how "random" your password is. More random = higher entropy = harder to crack. Just remember these benchmarks:

  • 40 bits以下 / Below 40 bits: 弱弱的 / Weak
  • 40-60 bits: 还行吧 / Decent
  • 60-80 bits: 挺强的 / Strong
  • 80 bits以上: 非常强 / Very Strong

Q: 我的密码会被你们偷偷存起来吗？/ Do you store my passwords?

A: 不会！密码就在你自己的电脑上生成，我们服务器毛都碰不到。/ Nope! Passwords are generated on YOUR device—our servers never see them.

Q: 密码多久换一次比较好？/ How often should I change passwords?

A: 如果密码够强又没泄露过，不用瞎折腾频繁换。发现泄露了再换就行。/ If it's strong and hasn't leaked, don't bother changing frequently. Just swap it if you hear about a breach.
