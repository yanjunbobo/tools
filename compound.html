关于复利计算器 About This Calculator

复利（Compound Interest），也就是咱们常说的"利滚利"——本金产生的利息会加到本金里，下一个周期利息是在更大的本金上算的。爱因斯坦老爷子据说都夸过复利是"世界第八大奇迹"，足见它的威力有多夸张 😄

Compound Interest, or "interest on interest" — the interest you earn gets added back to your principal, so you earn interest on a growing base. Einstein allegedly called it the "eighth wonder of the world"!

📐 复利公式 The Formulas

一次性复利终值 / Lump Sum Future Value：

F = P × (1 + r)n

F = 到期总资产 / Final Value，P = 本金 / Principal，r = 月利率 / Monthly Rate，n = 月数 / Months

每月定投终值 / DCA Future Value：

F = PMT × [(1 + r)n - 1] / r

PMT = 每月定投金额 / Monthly Investment Amount

🤔 复利 vs 单利 Compound vs Simple Interest

单利（Simple Interest）：只有本金生利息，利息花掉就不再增值了。举个例子👇

10万本金、年利率8%、放20年：收益 = 10万 × 8% × 20 = 16万。

Total: 10万 + 16万 = 26万。

复利（Compound Interest）：本金和利息一起滚雪球！同样是10万、年8%、20年：

终值 ≈ 46.6万，收益 ≈ 36.6万。比单利多出20万+！

——就因为利息也生了利息，这差距也太大了吧 😱

Simple Interest only applies to the principal. Compound Interest applies to both principal AND accumulated interest — that's the magic!

Example: 100,000 CNY at 8% for 20 years — Simple gives ~260K, Compound gives ~466K. That's 200K more!

💡 计算示例 Worked Examples

示例1 / Example 1：一次性投入10万，年化8%，20年后是多少？

Invest 100K lump sum, 8% annual return, 20 years — what's the result?

👉 答案 / Answer：10万 × (1 + 8%)20 ≈ 46.6万，收益约 / returns ≈ 36.6万！

示例2 / Example 2：每月定投1000元，年化8%，20年后是多少？

DCA 1,000 CNY/month, 8% annual return, 20 years?

👉 答案 / Answer：约 / ≈ 494万！其中本金 / principal = 24万，收益 / returns ≈ 470万！

这就是定投+复利的威力——Time is your best friend ⏰

🎯 定投为什么有效？Why Does DCA Work?

定投（Dollar-Cost Averaging）是一种"傻傻买"的策略——定期定额，不管市场涨跌。

DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging) means investing a fixed amount regularly, regardless of market conditions.

市场跌了：同样1000块能买到更多份额，摊低成本 📉

Market dips: same money buys more shares, lowering your average cost

市场涨了：账面盈利，心情好 😊

Market rises: unrealized gains feel great

长期坚持下来，成本会被自动摊平，再加上复利效应，收益相当可观。基金定投被认为是最适合普通人的投资方式之一，不需要择时，不用盯盘，省心！

Over time, DCA smooths out your cost basis. Add compound interest on top, and the results speak for themselves. It's why many consider DCA one of the best strategies for everyday investors!
