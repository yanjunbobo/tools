📖 关于复利计算器About This Calculator

复利（Compound Interest）Compound Interest，也就是咱们常说的"利滚利"——本金产生的利息会加到本金里，下一个周期利息是在更大的本金上算的。爱因斯坦老爷子据说都夸过复利是"世界第八大奇迹"，足见它的威力有多夸张 😄, or "interest on interest" — the interest you earn gets added back to your principal, so you earn interest on a growing base. Einstein allegedly called it the "eighth wonder of the world"!

📐 复利公式The Formulas

一次性复利终值：Lump Sum Future Value:

F = P × (1 + r)n

F = 到期总资产，P = 本金，r = 期利率，n = 期数F = Final Value, P = Principal, r = Periodic Rate, n = Number of Periods

每月定投终值：DCA Future Value:

F = PMT × [(1 + r)n - 1] / r

PMT = 每月定投金额PMT = Monthly Investment Amount

🤔 复利 vs 单利Compound vs Simple Interest

单利（Simple Interest）：Simple Interest:只有本金生利息，利息花掉就不再增值了。举个例子👇 only applies to the principal — you earn interest on your initial deposit, but not on the interest itself.

10万本金、年利率8%、放20年：收益 = 10万 × 8% × 20 = 100,000 CNY at 8% for 20 years → Returns = 100,000 × 8% × 20 = 160,000 CNY.

Total: 10万 + 16万 = 26万。Total: 260,000 CNY.

复利（Compound Interest）：Compound Interest:本金和利息一起滚雪球！同样是10万、年8%、20年： applies to both principal AND accumulated interest — that's the magic!

终值 ≈ Same example with compound: Final Value ≈ 466,000 CNY，收益 ≈ 36.6万。比单利多出, returns ≈ 366,000 CNY. That's 200K+ more！ than simple interest! 😱

🎯 定投为什么有效？Why Does DCA Work?

定投（Dollar-Cost Averaging）是一种"傻傻买"的策略——DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging) means investing a fixed amount regularly, regardless of market conditions — a "set and forget" strategy.

市场跌了：同样1000块能买到更多份额，摊低成本 📉Market dips: same money buys more shares, lowering your average cost 📉

市场涨了：账面盈利，心情好 😊Market rises: unrealized gains feel great 😊
